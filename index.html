Event Management SaaS ● Live in Production

NammaEventz

A full-ecosystem booking platform connecting customers with event service providers

NammaEventz solves a fragmented market problem: finding and booking trustworthy event vendors typically means juggling dozens of disconnected contacts. I designed and built a single digital ecosystem where customers discover, compare, and book photographers, caterers, decorators, makeup artists, and full event planners — while vendors manage their business through a dedicated dashboard.

The Challenge

Event services in the region were fragmented across word-of-mouth referrals and informal listings, with no unified way for customers to compare providers or for vendors to manage bookings and inquiries at scale.

My Solution

A subscription-based marketplace architecture with secure authentication, vendor profile management, and an integrated payment flow — built to scale from a regional launch to a multi-category platform covering 9+ service verticals.

Vendor discovery & comparison across 9+ categories
End-to-end service booking system
Customer inquiry management pipeline
Razorpay-integrated subscription billing
Admin dashboard for platform oversight
Mobile-friendly, fully responsive experience
🚀 Business impact: Brings customers and event service providers onto one platform, improving vendor visibility and cutting the friction out of service discovery and booking.

School Management ERP ● Live in Production

SchoolSync ERP

A unified digital platform for academic, administrative, and communication workflows

Schools typically run admissions, attendance, academics, and parent communication through disconnected paper trails and spreadsheets. SchoolSync ERP replaces that with one centralized system serving four distinct stakeholders — administrators, teachers, students, and parents — including a dedicated mobile app for teachers.

The Problem

Manual, paper-based processes across admissions, attendance, and academic record-keeping created administrative overhead and weak communication between school staff and parents.

The Solution

A role-based ERP system with web dashboards for administrators and a native Flutter mobile app for teachers — built on a secure, cloud-deployed architecture that keeps every stakeholder on one source of truth.

Student admission & data management
Faculty and attendance management
Academic records & timetable management
Notifications, announcements & messaging
Role-based authentication for 4 user types
Native teacher mobile app (Flutter)
🚀 Business impact: Cuts manual paperwork, centralizes school administration, and strengthens communication between staff, students, and parents through one secure platform.
